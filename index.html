Wireframe to Live

CatholicSingles.com Complete Redesgin

Catholic Singles' had a very out dated design for their site ad for this project I was responsible for not only design of site but UI/UX and market research. To start the project I conducted a very through evaluation of the site and it's look compared to competitors. Tools such as Google Analytics, heat mapping, user personals and others were used to ensure design quality.

The next steps I undertook was Using this research I then made recommendation for the user intake process, search, and other site considerations. All of my suggestions were taken and I then drafted wireframes for every page and element of the site. PSD mock-up for desktop, tablet, and smartphone layouts were delivered along with some custom CSS for things like menu responsiveness.

Dynamic Homepage

Las Vegas ReLeaf HomePage

One of the first and largest medical marijuana dispensaries in Las Vegas, Las Vegas ReLeaf's website has always received a fair amount of traffic. Having designed the entire site from the very start of business and parts of their app (such as over 50 custom icons), I decided to revisit the layout and design of the homepage.

I wanted something that let a visitor understand where they where upon landing without reading and went with hero shot of their product. Las Vegas ReLeaf had requested an area for them to upload a custom image for specials prominently above the fold; that's how that happen. The hero area also has nifty horizontal parallax scrolling effect that made the product image appear to almost rotate.

Simple Flyer Style Site

SoundscapeCustoms.com - SquareSpace

A simple flyer style kind of site for a friend's small business. The business had absolutely no branding or any kind of web presence. I created the logo, built a easy to update site on SquareSpace and a Product image PSD template. These were all done during a single evening while also training SquareSpace and using the Product PSD template.

Website isn't groundbreaking however product images created from PSD template proved to drive business via social media sharing.

Video Feature

5Star Helicopter Tours - After Effects

This is a 8 second video clip that I created in After Effects was used by 5 Star Helicopter Tours as a billboard video on display at the Las Vegas airport. The clip was made by editing over 2 hours of Helicopter footage down to time while still telling a story to viewer. All of the shots required post production touch-up effects, such as camera shake reduction and color correction.

The video is loosely based on my design for the individual tour landing pages of the 5 Star Helicopter Tours website. Each tour page renders a full page hero image of the tour, while also having the header render transparent, showcasing what makes that tour stand out. So the video has the best and most exciting parts of the tour shown in first person to hopefully capture the viewers interest.
